# Fitnesss_-Dashboard
📌 Project Overview

This project is a comprehensive Fitness & Gym Management Dashboard developed in Power BI to help gym owners and managers monitor business performance, track memberships, analyze financial metrics, and gain health insights about members.

 Business Requirements

The dashboard addresses the following business needs:

1. Overall Business Performance
Total Clients
Total Trainers
Total Revenue
Total Expenses
Total Profit
2. Monthly Performance Tracking
Monthly Member Acquisition
Monthly Revenue Analysis
Monthly Expense Analysis
Monthly Profit Tracking
Revenue vs Expenses Comparison
3. Membership Status Tracking
Active Memberships
Expired Memberships
Membership Tier Analysis (Platinum, Gold, Silver)
Expiring Members Monitoring
4. Fitness & Health Calculator
BMI (Body Mass Index)
BMR (Basal Metabolic Rate)
TDEE (Total Daily Energy Expenditure)
Maintenance Calories
Weight Loss Calorie Recommendations
5. Member Profile Management
Personal Information
Membership Details
Membership Status
Health Metrics
 Dashboard Features

✔ Interactive Power BI Dashboard
✔ Financial Performance Analysis
✔ Membership Management Tracking
✔ Health & Fitness Calculator
✔ Dynamic KPI Cards
✔ Monthly Trend Analysis
✔ DAX Measures & Calculations
✔ User-Friendly Navigation

🛠 Tools Used
Power BI
Power Query
DAX
Data Modeling
Excel / CSV
📈 Key Insights
Monitor gym business growth in real-time.
Track active and expired memberships.
Analyze revenue, expenses, and profitability.
Calculate BMI, BMR, and TDEE for members.
Support data-driven decision making.
